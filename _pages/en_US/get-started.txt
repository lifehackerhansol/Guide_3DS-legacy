### Required Reading

If you have hacked your 3DS in the past, or you purchased your 3DS second-hand, it is possible that your console already has custom firmware. You should [check for CFW](https://3ds.hacks.guide/checking-for-cfw) before proceeding.
{: .notice--warning}

There are many different entrypoints. If any of them apply to you, you can follow that method.

System Version 1.0.0 - 11.15.0 with Freakyforms Deluxe: [Installing boot9strap (freakyhax)](installing-boot9strap-(freakyhax))

System Version 2.1.0: [Installing boot9strap (2xrsa)](installing-boot9strap-(2xrsa))

System Version 4.X.X or 6.X.X with a DS flashcard: [Installing boot9strap (MSET)](installing-boot9strap-(mset))

System Version 9.0.0 - 11.9.0 with Cubic Ninja: [Homebrew Launcher (Ninjhax2)](homebrew-launcher-(ninjhax2))
